PACMAN RULES
To mute press M 
To unmute press U
Press up,down,left,right to control pacman. ( yellow point on the map ). 
Avoid Blue ghosts, eat food (the smallest points on the map) to get points. 
Eat  big red points to get extra 50 points.
After eating red points ghosts will change their color to cyan (bright blue). It means that you can eat them to get extra 100 points. It lasts about 6 seconds. After that ghosts immediately change their color to blue and are capable to eat you. Be careful because you won't get any warnings about their transform!
BE CAREFULL!After eating the ghost all of them will automaticly restored to their defualt positions. 
Green points teleport you to another green point.
You can't win. Main aim this game is to get as many points as it is possible.
